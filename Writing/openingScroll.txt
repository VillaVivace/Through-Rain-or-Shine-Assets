It is the year 2185 a viral disease outbreak has occurred after global warming melted the permafrost containing an ancient, unidentifiable virus. A few months have passed after the initial outbreak and most of the population is dead. There are rumors of a mysterious mail courier who delivers mail in the desecrate post-apocalyptic world. The courier’s goal is unknown, but through this one person, many find closure for the things they regret and hope that will help them move forward.
